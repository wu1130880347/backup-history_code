设置动态旋转角度增量。
  NAME
         axlSetRotateIncrement - Sets the dynamicsrotate angle increment.
  FUNCTION
         axlSetRotateIncrement (
             ?angular f_angular
             ?radial  f_radial
         )
         ==> t/nil
  SYNOPSIS
         Sets the dynamic rotateangle increment in degrees (f_angular)
         or radians (f_radial).  Sets the rotate increment forrotation
         of objects in the dynamicbuffer.
  NEEDS  
        f_angular        - Angle Lock increment in degrees.
        f_radial         - Radial lock increment in radians.
  RETURNS
         t/nil: t ifsuccessful; nil otherwise
  EXAMPLES
         The following example setsthe dynamic rotate angle to 15
         degrees:
                 axlSetRotateIncrement(?angular 15) ==> t
