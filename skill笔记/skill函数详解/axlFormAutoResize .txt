根据所需大小自动调整窗体大小
  NAME
         axlFormAutoResize - Resizes a form tofit its controls
  FUNCTION
         axlFormAutoResize(
             r_form
         )
         ==> t/nil
  SYNOPSIS
         Recalculates the requiredwidth and height and resizes the formbased
         on the current visibilityof the form's fields.
  NEEDS
         r_form          Handle for the form
  RETURNS
         t      Form was resized
         nil    r_form does not reference a valid form
