  NAME
         axlClearObjectCustomColor
  FUNCTION
         axlClearObjectCustomColor(
                 [lo_dbid]
                 )
         ==> t/nil
  SYNOPSIS
         Clear custom color of dbids
  NEEDS
         lo_dbid:        List of dbids to clear custom color.
  RETURNS
