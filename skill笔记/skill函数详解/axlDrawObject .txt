绘制dbid的列表。重绘由axlEraseObject被删除的任何对象。
  NAME
         axlDrawObject - Draw a list of AXL DBID's
  FUNCTION
         axlDrawObject(
                 lo_dbid
                 )
         ==> t/nil
  SYNOPSIS
         Processes a list of DBIDs .
         Redraws any objects thatwere erased by axlEraseObject.
  NEEDS
         lo_dbid:        List of DBIDS or one DBID.
  RETURNS
         t:      Something was drawn.
         nil:    No valid DBIDs or all objects already at desired display state.
  EXAMPLES
        None.
